Troubleshooting

Won‚Äôt Turn On

  1. Plug in the charger.
  2. Hold the power button.
  3. Charge for one hour.
  4. Perform a hard reboot: Hold Refresh + Power.
  5. If it still does not turn on, bring it to Room 231.

Only Works Plugged In

  1. Leave the Chromebook plugged in for one hour.
  2. Check to see if the battery percentage increases.
  3. If not, the battery may need service. Bring it to Room 231.

Flashing Yellow Light

  1. Leave the Chromebook charging overnight.
  2. If the light continues to flash, bring it to Room 231.

Safety & Care Reminders

Chromebooks are fragile. Treat them better than you would treat your own computer.

Screens crack easily, so always protect your device inside your bag.

Always keep the case on to protect the device.
