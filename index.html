How to Get Help

Follow these simple steps to request support or repair.

  1. Submit the Tech Support Form (In Spanish).
  2. Tell your teacher that you’ve submitted the form.
  3. Watch your student email for updates.
  4. If needed, you'll receive a loaner Chromebook.

Troubleshooting

Won’t Turn On

  1. Plug in the charger.
  2. Hold the power button.
  3. Charge for one hour.
  4. Hard reboot: Hold Refresh + Power.
  5. If still dead, fill out the form and go to Room 231.

Only Works Plugged In

  1. Keep it plugged in for an hour.
  2. Check if battery % increases.
  3. If not, battery needs service go to Room 231.

Flashing Yellow Light

  1. Leave it charging overnight and check in the morning.
  2. If still flashing, fill out the form and bring it to Room 231.

Safety & Care Reminders

Chromebooks are fragile. Screens crack easily so protect your device inside your bag.
